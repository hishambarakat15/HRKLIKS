HRKlix Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to HRKlix, a Human Resources Management System (HRMS). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and business data.

2. Information We Collect

HRKlix collects the following types of information:

  • *Personal Data*: Name, email, phone number, and job role.
  • *Employee Data*: Attendance records, payroll details, and performance evaluations.
  • *Company Data*: Business name, location, and HR policies.
  • *Technical Data*: IP address, browser type, and usage logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

HRKlix uses your data to:

  • Provide and manage HR services such as payroll, attendance tracking, and leave management.
  • Improve our platform and user experience.
  • Ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Send important updates and notifications.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement advanced security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our security practices include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data.
  • Secure server infrastructure.
  • Access controls and authentication measures.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

HRKlix does *not* sell or share your data with third parties except:

  • When required by law or government authorities.
  • With trusted service providers who help us operate the platform.
  • In case of business mergers or acquisitions.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services. Upon request, you may:

  • Access or update your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

HRKlix uses cookies to improve website functionality and user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if desired.

8. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request details about how we handle your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Complain to data protection authorities if you believe your rights have been violated.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

HRKlix reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or platform notifications.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Sales@hrklix.
